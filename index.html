About Me


Just an English guy living the dream in the United States of America. I'm a motivated designer and developer with experience creating custom websites with HTML, CSS and JavaScript. I have very strong collaboration skills and a proven history of application development. I am goal orientated and focused with a drive to succeed. Passionate about building world class websites and applications as well as awesome tracks for people to bob their heads to!

I have over 25 years of experience in the information technology field, having worked on/around computer systems, either for work or recreational reasons. I have used various production software both graphical and musical, and have acquired good knowledge working within both styles.

I have studied web development, where I learned various computer languages and worked within teams, as well as on my own, to create custom websites and applications.

I am a PADI Dive Master and earned my certificate in 2018 after completing multiple PADI courses, which were needed for me to obtain the certification. The most challenging, yet exciting of these courses being the PADI Rescue Diver. This required me to be trained in CPR, Primary and Secondary First Aid. It has given me the ability to notice and diver(s) in difficulty, in or out of the water and retrieve a person/body should an accident occur. Communication skills are very important in this field and from the start these skills were being developed and improved.

I have 10 years experience working within the construction industry, dealing with the layout of safety zones for utilities workers and implementing temporary traffic control to allow for a safe work environment. Attention to detail is very important and these skills were also being constantly developed and improved.

I think with my work ethic, focus and practical skills, I would be a valuable member of any team, and would bring along skills that I have developed through many hours of hard work and training.
